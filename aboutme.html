My Story

Hello, my name is Christopher Mattila. I am a music artist and freelance producer.

About Me

Coming from a family of music lovers, it is no surprise that I have loved music from a very young age. I can't remember a time when I wasn't fiddling on the piano, banging away on the drums, trying desperately to figure out chords on the guitar, or performing shameless karaoke along with whatever song was playing. I grew up watching my father write and record his own songs, playing every instrument he could get his hands on, so I have built a very sincere appreciation of the musical environment and everything it has to offer.

After relocating to Victoria, B.C., I now enjoy collaborating, recording, and performing music in a variety of settings. My personal philosophy is that music is best shared in an open and fun production and performance space. Ensuring my clients feel comfortable and open during the production and recording process is of utmost importance to me. I love exploring the deeper meanings of music alongside appreciating its most delicate simplicities.

While recording and producing several personal projects in various studios and public spaces around the city, I realized that every room, pub, stage, and café has something uniquely magical to offer. Music has the profound ability to bring people together and bonds the producer, performer, and audience as one.

Styles & Motivation

I enjoy combining music's traditional styles, like classical, jazz, and R&B, with the unique styles and experiences my clients bring. This creates an authentic atmosphere for the artist that helps them stand out while remaining true to the integrity of their music's foundations. I personally love to mix the stories heard in blues and gospel with the modern sounds of rock, folk, and electronic.

My passion for music and the unique sense of satisfaction that comes with bringing others joy has led me to where I am today. Providing every single one of my clients with a truly customizable and unique experience tailored specifically for them is an extremely rewarding and fulfilling experience.

I can't wait to work with you and create something truly magical.
