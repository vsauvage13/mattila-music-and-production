I am Christopher Mattila, and I am the founder-operator of Mattila Music and Production. I offer freelance music and production services in beautiful Victoria, B.C.

I believe that all music, whether shared in the smallest room, the most hole-in-the-wall pub, or the largest stage, has something unique and magical to offer. My passion for music helps me to capture this magic and inspires me to perform, produce, and collaborate with others to create the most beautiful and captivating written and recorded works.

With Mattila Music, you can be sure that every effort is made to accurately capture and translate the energy and emotion your music holds into the finished product. I cannot wait to hear what magic you have in store.
