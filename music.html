My Music

I grew up in an incredibly musical environment, and I remain inspired by that today. I honestly cannot remember a time when I wasn't fiddling on one instrument or another, trying to figure out the chords and melodies to my favourite songs. My passion for music has only grown since then, and I am immensely proud of the musician I have become.

I believe that music is a uniquely beautiful part of our world and I am especially awed by its ability to bring humans together to share such deep and raw emotions that we would not usually feel without it. Regardless of my role in creating music, whether it be producer, mixer, performer, or songwriter, I always strive to bring this philosophy into my work so that others can share in music's unique and beautiful energy.

All of my original songs and covers, which are listed here, share a piece of me that I hope can inspire others to take hold of their creative dreams, whatever they may be.
