We also specialize in capturing performances at a broad variety of performance and recording spaces. We believe that each space has something unique to offer and taking advantage of the spaces sound and atmosphere is essential to capturing the same sound from the artist in that space.

A vocalist in a large church hall captures the boldness of the artist's instrument through reverberation and sound deflection. In contrast, the raw sound of an artist or instrument in an outdoor setting captures its direct essence in relation to its environment through dead sound waves and external live sounds out of the artists control.

Mastering

$50-$200/Song

Mastering is the final step of song production. We take the audio files you give us, optimize them for playback on any system and media format using tools such as equalization, compression, limiting, and stereo enhancement.
