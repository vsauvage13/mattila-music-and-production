Heart of Stone Remastered

  1. Freelance Projects
  2. Heart of Stone Remastered

Heart of Stone Remastered. This project was completed using Ableton Live Digital Audio Workstation.

A couple of clients came to me looking for help with a cover project they were doing for one of their favourite musicals. This was my first collaboration project with them and gave me an opportunity to work together with them for the first time.

I predomidantly helped with mixing for this project. I begin with recording live takes of both clients instrumentation. After we got the perfect recording of instrumentation we recorded the vocal takes including the main melody and harmony. Then after I had all of the tracks recorded to my standard, I engineered the mix in Ableton Live Digital Audio Workstation and worked on equalization and other effects to boost the quality. I quantized the mix to ensure rhythmic precision for an overall more pleasant sounding mix.
